Joint Commission International (JCI)

24/7 MD

is accredited by the Joint Commission International (JCI), the world’s most prestigous accrediting body of healthcare organizatons. JCI is the international arm of the Joint Commission on Accreditation of Healthcare Organization (JCAHO), the oldest and most prestigious healthcare accreditation institution in the world. It is an independent, non-profit organization based in the United States that evaluates and accredits healthcare organizations and programs worldwide.
